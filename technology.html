AI Pipeline

From Raw Data to
Actionable Insight

Every AI model follows a rigorous machine learning pipeline to ensure accuracy, reliability, and continuous improvement.

1

Raw Data Collection

Agricultural data gathered from IoT sensors, satellite imagery, government databases, and farmer reports across 50+ datasets.

2

Data Cleaning & Validation

Automated pipelines remove outliers, fill missing values, and validate data integrity using domain-specific agricultural rules.

3

Feature Engineering

Domain experts identify key predictive features. Derived variables like NPK ratios, climate indices, and seasonal factors are computed.

4

Model Training

Ensemble models trained with cross-validation and hyperparameter tuning. Distributed training on cloud GPUs for deep learning models.

5

Model Evaluation

Rigorous testing on held-out datasets. A/B testing against baseline models. Human expert review of edge cases.

6

Deployment & Monitoring

Blue-green deployments with automated rollback. Real-time model drift detection and continuous retraining pipelines.
